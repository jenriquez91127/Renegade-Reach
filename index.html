Bakersfield College Renegade Reach Privacy Policy

No Data Collection

Renegade Reach does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your conversations with Ren are not recorded, saved, or associated with your student account, identity, or any BC system.

Session Confidentiality

Every session begins fresh. When you close or refresh the page, all conversation content is permanently discarded. No history is retained between sessions, and no third party has access to what you share with Ren.

Not a Clinical Service

Renegade Reach is a peer wellness support tool, not a licensed clinical or counseling service. It does not constitute therapy, diagnosis, or medical advice. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact the BC Counseling Center, call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline), or dial 911.

Voluntary Use

Use of Renegade Reach is entirely voluntary. You may exit the conversation at any time by closing or refreshing the page. No record of your visit to this page is linked to your BC student profile.

Cookies & Tracking

This page does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or any third-party monitoring tools. Your visit is not logged or analyzed.

Renegade Reach is a student wellness initiative developed at Bakersfield College. For questions about this policy, contact the BC Office of Student Services.  ·  Bakersfield College © 2025 Kern Community College District.
