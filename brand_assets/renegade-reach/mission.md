# Renegade Reach — Mission

## What It Is
Renegade Reach is a student wellness initiative at Bakersfield College, built by BC students for BC students.

## The Problem It Solves
BC students carry overlapping burdens:
- Financial pressure
- Family expectations and obligations
- Identity struggles (first-gen, cultural, generational)
- Academic stress

Walking into a counseling office feels like a burden — stigmatized, logistically hard, emotionally exposing. Most students suffer in silence rather than seek help.

## The Solution
A chatbot named **Ren** gives every BC student a confidential, comforting, deep-thinking talk space — available the moment they need it, with zero barrier to entry.

## The Initiative
- Built by BC students, for BC students
- Lives as a dedicated page on the Bakersfield College website
- Not a landing page. Not a marketing site. A functional wellness tool.
- Lowers the barrier to mental wellness and deep thinking
