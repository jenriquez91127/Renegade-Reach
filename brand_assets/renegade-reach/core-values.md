# Renegade Reach — Core Values

## 1. Accessibility
- No appointment required
- No waitlist
- No referral needed
- Available 24/7 — the moment a student needs it
- Zero cost, zero friction

## 2. Confidentiality
- Every conversation starts fresh — a new Ren bot each session
- No conversation history is stored or shared
- Students can speak freely without fear of judgment or record
- Confidentiality is not just a feature — it is the foundation of trust

## 3. Student-Centricity
- Built by BC students who understand the lived experience
- Language, tone, and approach reflect real student life at BC
- Not clinical. Not corporate. Peer-adjacent in warmth.

## 4. Emotional Safety
- Ren warms up before going deeper — earns the right to ask harder questions
- Never pushes. Never diagnoses. Never dismisses.
- Creates a container for honest expression before offering any depth

## 5. Dignity
- Every student deserves to be heard
- Suffering in silence is not strength — Ren gives students an alternative
- The experience respects the weight of what students are carrying
