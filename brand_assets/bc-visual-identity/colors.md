# Bakersfield College — Official Color Palette

## Primary Colors

| Name            | Pantone     | Hex       | RGB              | Usage                          |
|-----------------|-------------|-----------|------------------|--------------------------------|
| BC Crimson Red  | PMS 187 C   | #A6192E   | rgb(166, 25, 46) | Logo, primary accents, buttons |
| Process Black   | PMS Black C | #000000   | rgb(0, 0, 0)     | Text, outlines, secondary use  |
| White           | —           | #FFFFFF   | rgb(255,255,255) | Backgrounds, reversed type     |

## Notes
- PMS 187 C is a deep crimson — NOT a bright red. It leans toward burgundy.
- The logo mark is described as "red with black outline."
- White is a true secondary color (not just a background) — the Renegades are "red and white."
- No official tertiary or accent colors have been identified in public brand documents.
- For digital use, #A6192E is the closest web equivalent to PMS 187 C.
- Some sources cite #AC1A2F or #A9172A as alternate conversions — all are within the same deep crimson range.

## Do Not Use
- Bright/neon reds (e.g. #FF0000)
- Purple, blue, gold, or other colors not from the BC palette
- Any color not approved by BC Marketing & Public Relations (web@bakersfieldcollege.edu)
